# Google Play Store Upload Guide (Play Store Par App Mukvani Rit)

Ek var tamare **`.aab` file** bani jay ane **Server/Database Online** thai jay, pachi niche mujab na steps follow karo.

## Step 1: Google Play Console Account ($25)
Jo tamari pase account nathi, to [Google Play Console](https://play.google.com/console/) par jao.
- Google ID thi login karo.
- **$25 (Andaje â‚¹2100)** ni one-time fee bharvi padshe.
- ID Verification karavu padshe (Pan Card / Driving License thi).

## Step 2: Create App (Navi App Banavo)
1. Console Dashboard par **"Create App"** button dabavo.
2. **App Name**: `Divya Darshan` (ke je rakhvu hoy).
3. **Default Language**: `Gujarati` athva `English`.
4. **App or Game**: `App`.
5. **Free or Paid**: `Free`.
6. **Declarations**: Banne checkbox tick karo ane "Create App" dabavo.

## Step 3: Set up your app (Basic Setup)
Dashboard par "Set up your app" section hase. Aa badha steps line-sar pura karva padshe:

1. **Privacy Policy**: 
   - Tamari privacy policy ni link mukvi padshe.
   - (Jo na hoy, to "Privacypolicies.com" par free ma banavi shako cho).
   
2. **App Access**: 
   - Select: **"All functionality is available without special access"** (Jo login jaruri na hoy).
   - Jo login farajiyat hoy, to **"Restricted parts available"** select karo ane ek demo login aapo (Test User).

3. **Ads**: 
   - Select: **"No, my app does not contain ads"**.

4. **Content Rating**: 
   - "Start Questionnaire" dabavo.
   - Email id nakho.
   - Category: "Social" athva "Utility" select karo.
   - Vadhu padta prashno ma "No" aavshe (Violence, Sex, Gambling vagere).
   - Save karo.

5. **Target Audience**: 
   - **"18 and over"** select karo (Safety mate).
   - "Appeal to children?" -> **"No"**.

6. **News Apps**: 
   - **"No"**.

7. **Data Safety (Mahatvanu)**: 
   - Tame user no data lo cho? -> **"Yes"**.
   - Shu data encrypted che? -> **"Yes"**.
   - Select karo: **Name, Phone Number** (App functionality mate).

## Step 4: Store Listing (Dekhav)
Side menu ma **"Main store listing"** ma jao.
- **App Name**: Divya Darshan
- **Short Description**: Live Darshan and Temple Guide App.
- **Full Description**: App vishe lakho.
- **Graphics Upload**:
  - **App Icon**: 512x512 pixels (PNG).
  - **Feature Graphic**: 1024x500 pixels.
  - **Phone Screenshots**: 2 thi 8 photo upload karo.

## Step 5: Publish (App Upload)
Have tamari `.aab` file upload karvani che.

1. Side menu ma **Production** shodho.
2. **"Create new release"** par click karo.
3. **App Integrity**: "Change signing key" -> "Use Google-generated key" (Recommended).
4. **App Bundles**: Tamari `.aab` file ahi drag & drop karo.
5. Upload thai jay etle niche **Release Name** ma version (e.g., 1.0.0) aavi jashe.
6. **"Next"** dabavo.
7. Jo koi Error na hoy, to **"Save"** ane **"Go to Overview"**.
8. Chhelle, **"Send changes for review"** button dabavo.

**Bus! Have Google tamari app check karshe (1-3 divas thai shake) ane pachi Play Store par Live thai jashe.**
